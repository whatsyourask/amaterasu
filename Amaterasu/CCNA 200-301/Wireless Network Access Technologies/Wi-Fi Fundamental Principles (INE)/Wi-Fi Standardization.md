[[What is Wi-Fi?]]
* Standardization done by the IEEE 802.11 working group.
* Standardization broken into 2 sub-groups:
	* Physical layer
	* MAC (Media Access Control Layer)
* Letter after 802.11 represents an amendment to the original Wi-Fi standard:
	* 802.11
	* 802.11b
	* 802.11n
